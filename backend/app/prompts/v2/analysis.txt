You are analyzing a {timeframe} {mode} trading chart screenshot.
Determine if price is CONTINUING its trend or REVERSING. Do NOT default to trend continuation.

═══ STEP 1 — TREND & PATTERN ═══
Identify the prevailing trend from swing highs/lows (UP/DOWN/RANGE).
Check the last 1-3 candles for reversal patterns (engulfing, doji, pin bar, shooting star, hammer).
Output: trend (UP/DOWN/RANGE), pattern (name or NONE), pattern_signal (BULLISH/BEARISH/NONE)

═══ STEP 2 — LEVELS & MOMENTUM ═══
Is price at a key support/resistance level? Was there rejection or breakout?
Is momentum exhausting (shrinking bodies, growing wicks)?
Output: level_signal (BULLISH/BEARISH/NEUTRAL), momentum_signal (BULLISH_EXHAUSTION/BEARISH_EXHAUSTION/NONE)

═══ STEP 3 — STRUCTURE & DECISION ═══
Has there been a break of structure (close beyond last swing high/low)?
Combine all signals. At least 2 must agree for a directional call.
If signals conflict, output NEUTRAL with confidence 35-50.
Output: structure_break (BULLISH_BREAK/BEARISH_BREAK/NONE), final direction, confidence

CONFIDENCE CALIBRATION:
- 85-95: Strong reversal pattern + level rejection + structure break (all 3 agree)
- 70-84: Two signals agree clearly
- 55-69: One clear signal, others neutral
- 35-54: Mixed or unclear signals → must output NEUTRAL

Output ONLY a raw JSON object with these keys:
currentPrice, direction (BUY/SELL/NEUTRAL), marketTrend (Bullish/Bearish/Neutral/Ranging),
confidence (0-100), isReversal (boolean), reasoning (array of 3 strings for each step),
pattern, volume, momentum, timeInTrade,
supportLevels (array of 2 numbers), resistanceLevels (array of 2 numbers),
orderBlocks (array of {type:'bullish'|'bearish', from:number, to:number})
